# Training Guide

## Training Objectives

- Equip SBA administrators to manage users, data governance, and reporting
- Equip county and field teams to capture high-quality business records
- Equip analysts and partners to interpret dashboards and reports
- Equip technical staff to deploy, back up, restore, and maintain the system

## Role-Based Training Sessions

### Session 1: SBA Leadership and Program Managers

- Duration: 2 hours
- Topics:
  - executive dashboard
  - policy and partner reporting
  - county progress monitoring
  - approval and oversight responsibilities

### Session 2: SBA Admin and County Supervisors

- Duration: 4 hours
- Topics:
  - user roles and permissions
  - MSME and BDSP validation
  - workflow transitions
  - notifications and audit review

### Session 3: Data Entry Officers and Inspectors

- Duration: 1 day practical
- Topics:
  - MSME registration
  - BDSP registration
  - verification visits
  - offline capture
  - sync conflict handling
  - document upload rules

### Session 4: Analysts and Development Partners

- Duration: 3 hours
- Topics:
  - analytics interpretation
  - report generation
  - exports
  - approved/public data boundaries

### Session 5: IT and System Administrators

- Duration: 1 day technical
- Topics:
  - deployment
  - environment variables
  - backups and restore
  - logging and troubleshooting
  - production hardening priorities

## Practical Exercises

1. Create a new MSME record and submit it for review
2. Verify an MSME and move it to approved status
3. Register a BDSP and add a support record to an MSME
4. Save an offline draft and sync it to the server
5. Generate a county report and download PDF and XLSX versions
6. Review audit entries related to a sample approval

## Training Materials Recommended

- Projector-based admin walkthrough
- Tablet-based field simulation
- Printed quick reference sheets for county teams
- Sandbox environment with seed data
